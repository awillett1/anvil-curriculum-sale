Volunteers - Anvil Curriculum Sale

June 12, 2026 / 10 AM - 2 PM / Anvil's Cumming Campus

Volunteers

Volunteer Opportunities

Before

  • Set-Up
    • Help sort through donations and put them at the correct tables.
    • Help people dropping off to know where to put their items.
  • Arrange tables by category (math, science, literature, etc.)
  • Provide signage for easy navigation.
  • Set-up a check-in/check-out area with QR codes.

During

  • Keep the area tidy and restock as needed during the sale.

After

  • Clean-Up
    • Gathering leftover items and resetting the room.
    • Light sweeping.
    • Trash removal.
  • Managing leftover sale items. This includes helping sellers gather unsold items and also transporting donations to Goodwill.
