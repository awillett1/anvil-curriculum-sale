Sellers - Anvil Curriculum Sale

June 12, 2026 / 10 AM - 2 PM / Anvil's Cumming Campus

Sellers

Seller Guidelines

  • Each seller will be assigned a unique seller number when you register.
  • Sellers should ensure all materials are in good, usable condition.
  • On the sign-up form, please confirm if you would like to donate unsold items or if you will be picking them up at the designated time.

Payment Process

  • Every item must be clearly labeled with your seller number and marked with a price.
  • Bring a full page (8.5 x 11 inches) printed copy of your Venmo QR code to display for payment when droppin off items.
  • Buyers will pay sellers directly by using the seller numbers to locate their Venmo QR code. For simplicity, this is the only form of payment we will use and no cash will be exchanged.

Inventory for Sellers

  • Clearly label all items before the designated drop-off period. Place items on the correct table (will be organized by subject).
  • Please do not drop off items ahead of time - deliver them during the designated drop-off time only. Only donated items will be accepted prior to the sale.
